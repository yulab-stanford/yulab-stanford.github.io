Victoria Fan

Research Associate


Department of Ophthalmology

Stanford School of Medicine

Victoria Fan completed her undergraduate training at Stanford University, where her work primarily involved developing new technologies for treating vision loss. After graduating in 2018, she joined the Yu Lab to assist in the development of an electronic corneal prosthesis pioneered by the lab. Prior to that, she was actively involved in the Stanford Artificial Retina Project.
